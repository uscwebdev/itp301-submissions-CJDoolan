Product SC

I am a part of the USC club, Innovation Design. In this club, I was tasked with redesigning a club logo (ProductSC) and creating a new brand identity. The first step in the process was researching the club's values, mission, and target audience, as well as the broader market context in which the club operates. With this information, I was able to develop a logo that represented the club's unique identity and resonated with its audience. This logo could then be integrated into a broader brand identity, which included colors, typography, imagery, and messaging. I worked to create a cohesive and memorable brand that could stand out in a competitive market, attract new members, and strengthen the club's relationship with its existing ones.

Problem Statement

Revamp and rebrand the "Product SC" club with a team of four designers, aiming to create a fresh and captivating visual identity that resonates with the club's mission and values. The rebrand should encompass a new logo, color scheme, website, and promotional materials, with the primary goal of attracting and engaging a broader and more diverse audience, including students and professionals interested in product management and related fields.

Product SC

Product SC's Instagram page underwent a significant transformation. Previously, the page lacked a cohesive visual identity making it less appealing. The content posted on the page lacked consistency in terms of color schemes, typography, and imagery, making it hard for followers to identify with the brand. However, with the help of our design team, the page underwent a significant revamp. My team and I created visually appealing Instagram templates that incorporated the company's brand colors, typography, and imagery, creating a cohesive and recognizable visual identity. The templates used on the page allowed for consistency in the brand's messaging and design. As a result, the page has seen a considerable increase in engagement, with more followers interacting with and sharing the content.
