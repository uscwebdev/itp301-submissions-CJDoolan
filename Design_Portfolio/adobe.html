Spring Eukre Playing Cards

For this project, the objective was to delve into the principles of unity and cohesion by designing a set of playing cards. The task involves creating a cohesive visual language and thematic connection across all cards, jokers, and the card backs. The ultimate goal is to showcase our creative design skills and ability to maintain a consistent visual narrative throughout the entire card set.

Problem Statement

Create a visually appealing and informative pop-up banner design for Marshall Executive Education, ensuring it complies with the company's specified style and size guidelines. This banner aims to effectively promote the company's services and engage its target audience at various events and exhibitions.
